Editorial sections

JOSTA welcomes submissions across a broad range of subject areas related to sustainable technology in agriculture. Below are the defined editorial sections along with descriptions and relevant tags to help authors identify the most suitable category for their manuscript.

Crop and Plant Science

Focuses on advances in crop production, breeding, physiology, agronomy, and sustainable plant-based innovations, including aspects of plant health, entomology, pest and disease management, resistance mechanisms, and integrated pest and disease management (IPDM) strategies. Tags: Crops, PlantScience

Soil Science and Agroecology

Covers soil health, nutrient management, soil microbiology, and ecological approaches to farming systems. Tags: Soil, Ecology

Animal Science and Fisheries

Includes studies related to livestock management, aquaculture, animal health, nutrition, and breeding. Tags: Livestock, Fisheries

Biotechnology & Molecular Biology

Research in genetic engineering, genomics, molecular markers, tissue culture, and related biotechnology tools in agriculture. Tags: Biotech, Molecular

Agricultural Engineering and Technology

Covers machinery, irrigation systems, automation, sensors, precision agriculture, and farm energy innovations. Tags: Engineering, Technology

Data Science and Statistics in Agriculture

Focuses on the application of data analytics, statistical methods, AI, and modeling in agricultural research. Tags: Data, Statistics

Agricultural Economics and Policy

Includes research on farm economics, market analysis, price policy, rural livelihoods, and agribusiness. Tags: Economics, Policy

Extension Education and Rural Development

Studies on outreach methods, knowledge transfer, participatory learning, rural livelihoods, and agricultural communication. Tags: Extension, Rural

Post-Harvest Technology and Food Science

Addresses post-harvest handling, storage, food processing, value addition, and food quality. Tags: PostHarvest, Food

Climate Change and Sustainable Agriculture

Research on adaptation and mitigation strategies, carbon farming, climate-resilient agriculture, and sustainability metrics. Tags: Climate, Sustainability

Forestry and Agroforestry

Focuses on tree-based farming systems, biodiversity conservation, forest resource management, and ecosystem services. Tags: Forestry, Agroforestry

Library and Information Science

Focuses on the organization, management, and dissemination of agricultural knowledge through digital libraries, open-access systems, bibliometrics, and information literacy for research and innovation. Tags: Library, InformationScience
